Multimodal Stress Detection

Fuse facial emotion with binary text stress detection to estimate stress from webcam or uploaded images.

The browser captures webcam frames with getUserMedia, Flask predicts facial emotion, and the text model tells you clearly whether the person looks stressed or not stressed from the written input.
